Add new entries following the same format:
{path: '/home/linaro/web-player/video/your-video.mp4', volume: 1.0}


Supported Video Formats:
- MP4 with H.264 codec (recommended)
- MOV with H.264 codec
- Resolution: Up to 1920x1080
- Frame rate: 30fps (tested)
- Audio: AAC or MP3
